WE ARE LOOKING FOR A NEW BRAND AMBASSADOR
Are you super friendly, based in London and keen to join a team challenging the soft drinks industry? We’re looking for proactive individuals to represent the Dash mission to naturally hydrate the nation in a fun sustainable way.
You’ll be spreading our mission & introducing our drinks to new consumers. Who is Dash and what do you stand for? With current fridge space full of sugar and artificially filled drinks Dash is shaking up the norm.

Dash is British sparkling spring water infused with wonky fruit & veg (the ones which are rejected by the supermarkets because they’re misshapen) for a natural
dash of flavour with no sugar, calories or sweetener. Our mission is to naturally hydrate the nation and reduce food waste in a fun, sustainable way.
